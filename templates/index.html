📘 How to use AskYourPDF

  1. Upload a PDF – click the Upload button and select a text‑based PDF.
  2. Load a document – choose from the dropdown, then click Load.
  3. Ask questions – type your question and press Enter or click Send.
  4. Summarize – get a concise summary of the entire document.
  5. Print Summary – download the summary as a PDF file.
  6. Delete – remove a saved document permanently.
  7. Clear chat – wipe the conversation history.
  8. Language – switch the answer language (translation happens automatically).
